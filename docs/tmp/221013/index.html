    知识点

    在美国，CCTV = closed circuit television

    surveillance 监视

    -> you are under CCTV surveillance

    it is custom for sb. to do sth. 习惯干

    on the latch 掩着门

    a local term 当地的说法

    hold water 站得住脚

    tranquil = peaceful = quiet, calm

    era = epoch 时代

    close

    close up 关上门，暂时关闭

    -> Many shopping malls don't close up until 12 p.m. on Christmas Eve.

    close down 倒闭（= go bankrupt）

    -> The enterprise has closed down due to poor management.

    vulnerable

    vulnerable 无防御的，脆弱的

    be vulnerable to sth.

    -> vulnerability = Achilles's Heel

    statistics

    According to reliable statistics / figures / data 据可靠统计数据

    According to statistics released / provided by… 据……提供的数据

    Trans：据有关当局的统计数据显示，越来越多的中国年轻人对其婚姻不满意。

    -> According to statistics released by authorities concerned, an increasing/ a rising/a mounting number of young people in China are dissatisfied with their marriage.

    数据表明：statistics show / suggest / indicate / reflect / reveal / demonstrate / manifest / demonstrate / manifest + that

    allege

    allege that

    be alleged to do sth.

    Trans:美国一邪教组织声称他们已经克隆了人类。

    -> The evil cult in America alleged that they had cloned human beings.

    cult 异教，乌合之众

    上升

    increase 上升

    = rise

    = go up

    = climb

    = fly

    = boom

    = soar

    = ascend

    = mount

    猛增：+ greatly / sharply / abruptly / steeply / dramatically

    ascend 上升

    decend 下降

    decendant 后人

    transcend 超越

    越来越多

    more and more

    = a(n) increasing / rising / … number of

    hook up to

    hook up to 电器间的连接

    Trans: 和朋友联机打游戏。

    -> play games by hooking my pc up to my friend's.

    built-in

    built-in 内置的，内在的

    与生俱来的幽默感：

    built-in sense of humor

    = in-built sense of humor

    = inherent sense of humor

    = integral sense of humor

    pry

    撬开：pry sth open / pry sth away

    Trans：这个小偷撬开了防盗锁，简直是不可思议！但他就是得逞了！

    -> It is incredible that the burglar has pried the anti-theft lock, yet he just made it.

    窥探隐私：I don't mean to pry, but do you…

    Trans:狗仔队（paparazzo）依靠窥探明星的绯闻而赚钱.

    Paparazzi make money by prying into the stars' rosy/pink affairs.

    丑闻：scandal
