    Unit 3 Part 2 Text A My Misspent Youth

    知识点

    get by = lead a so-so life 设法活下去，勉强应对

    more… than… = not… but… 与其说……不如说……

    fallout （核爆炸后的）放射性沉降物；后果

    cloth cutting 刺绣

    sort out 理清

    a herd of = a group of

    free lancer 自由职业者

    kind of / sort of 多多少少有一点

    modest

    not very large or expensive 不大的，不很贵的，适度的

    The government has made modest progress in cutting greenhouse gas emissions. 政府在减少温室气体排放方面已有一些作为。

    • on a modest scale
    • a modest growth/increase
    • a modest proposal
    • a modest amount of

    the last… to do

    The last… to do

    = The last… + attributive clause

    = least likely 最不愿意；最不可能

    e.g. This is the last thing I would ever want to do.

    Analysis of "negative form indicates positive meaning"

    negative form indicates positive meaning

    01

    Trans: One can't be too honest. 人越老实越好。

    cannot/hardly/never… too… “无论怎样……也不过分”或“越……越好”。

    e.g. A man can never have too many friends.

    02

    It is a wise man that never makes mistakes. 无论多么聪明的人，也难免犯错误。

    It is a+ 形容词 + 名词 + that… never/not/no… 无论怎样的……也不……。不是强调句型，而具有含蓄的让步意味。

    e.g. It's a long lane that has no turning.

    03

    I'm too anxious to know the result.

    too glad/easy/ready/anxious/eager/willing/happy etc… to do sth

    = very, extremely

    e.g. We are only too happy to take care of your dog for the weekend.

    I am too eager to see my high school teachers and friends.

    04

    Trans: All that glitters is not gold. 闪光的东西不一定都是金子。

    不定代词 all, both, every…; 副词 always, often, entirely --used with not--

    Partial Negation “并非都是，不是每个人都”

    I don't remember all these formulas.

    05

    The mountain is not valuable because it is high. 山并不因为高而具有价值。

    In the adverbial clause of reason, when the main clause expresses a negative meaning, the meaning of this sentence is to negate the adverbial of reason“并不因为......而..…..…”

    e.g. Galileo was not ready to accept it just because Aristotle had said so.

    conviction

    vict = win

    convict 定罪

    conviction = firm belief

    strengthen one's conviction 坚定某人的信念

    Trans: 这位英勇的共产党员不顾敌人的种种诱惑以及对其实施的酷刑，从未曾动摇过自己的信仰。

    Despite(In spite of) the enemy's various temptations and cruel tortures the heroic communist would never shaken his conviction.

    负债

    over one's head 完全地

    be up to my neck/ears/eyes/eyebrows in debt

    be over one's head and ears in debt

    be deep in debt

    Be debt-ridden

    With a mountain of debts

    exert

    用力，使劲

    Trans:中国政府一直努力提高老百姓的获得感，幸福感和安全感。

    Chinese government has been exerting her strength to improve people's sense of gain, happiness and security.

    try/do my best to do sth.

    = do everything I can to do

    = spare no effort to do sth

    = work out one's heart to do

    = strive to do sth

    = do one's utmost

    emulate

    模仿

    Trans: J.K.罗琳有着与生俱来的写作天赋和想象力，因此极少有作家在写作的商业化成就上能赶上她。

    JK Rowing has a built-in writing gift and imagination,therefore, few writer can emulate her in their achievements in commercialized writing.

    yearn

    渴望

    Yearn for sth

    = long for

    = crave for sth

    = thirst for sth

    = be dying to do sth

    Trans：世界人民一直渴望研制出新冠肺炎特效药。

    People all over the world have been yearning for developing the wonder drug for Covid-19

    affluent

    affluent = wealthy 富裕的

    be affluent in sth. 丰富的

    build an affluent society

    affluent circumstances

    Trans:中东的石油储备非常丰富，这既给它带来了财富，也为其带来了社会动荡。

    The Middle East is rich/affluent/ample in oil reserves, which has brought about both wealth and social turmoil in this region.

    poverty

    as poor as a church mouse

    burn a hole in one's pocket 有钱就花，没有节制

    mortgage 贷款

    hedge

    1. 土篱笆
    2. 防备；缓冲

    hedge one's bets 留退路

    迷恋于

    be enamored of

    be obsessed with sth.

    be bewitched by sth.

    be enchanted by sth.
