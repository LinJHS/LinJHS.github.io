    Unit 2 Part 2 Text A The Land of the Lock

    知识点

    defend sb against 保护……免受伤害

    to put up barriers 设置障碍

    it occurs to sb 想到

    barricade sb against 将..与..隔绝开来

    to take over our lives 主宰我们的生活

    surveillance

    surveillance 监视

    = monitor

    keep sb. under surveillance

    Trans: 由于这所夜总会被控有非法活动，所以正处在警方的严密监视下。

    The night club has been accused of illegal activities, therefore, the police are keeping it under close surveillance.

    attachment

    attachment 附件

    be attached to / be hooked on sth. 喜欢，沉迷于

    feature

    This restaurant features vegetarian dishes.

    This exhibition featured various highly sophisticated internet products.

    This movie "Home Coming" features Zhongyi.

    Violence and romance feature in all action movies directed by Zhang Yimou.

    transform

    （trans = from … to）

    • transfuse（blood）
    • transplant（heart）
    • transsexual
    • transport
    • translate
    • transmit（values to sb.）
    • transgenetic（food）

    defend

    defend sb against 保护某人免受伤害

    Trans：为了不让敌人逼近，秦始皇下令修建了举世瞩目的万里长城。

    First Emperor of Qin has ordered to construct the Great Wall which has attracted worldwide attention with an attempt to hold enemies at bay.

    barrier

    build / erect / /form / set up / put up a barrier

    remove / overcome / break down / eliminate a barrier

    take over

    take over

    = control sb.

    overcome 打败

    overturn 推翻，（使）倾覆

    中西餐对比

    point-by-point

    1. various types
    2. more nutritious
    3. kinship

    可能

    there is every chance that...

    there is much possibility that...

    it may well be that...

    chances are that...

    the odds are that...

    Chances are that this conference will promote friendship and cultural exchange between China and other countries, and thus will boost/advance/facilitate/accelerate China's economy.

    hold/keep sb. at bay

    hold/keep sb. at bay 不让某人逼近；使某人走投无路

    Trans：为了不让敌人逼近，秦始皇下令修建了举世瞩目的万里长城。

    First Emperor of Qin has ordered to construct the Great Wall which has attracted worldwide attention with an attempt to hold enemies at bay.

    without so much as...

    = with not even

    连……也不；甚至于不（用于表示某人没有做应做的事）

    She left the city where she had lived all her life without so much as a backward glance.

    Trans:日本在侵略中国后连一句道歉的话也没有，完全否认历史，这个令我们非常气愤。

    Without so much as a word of apology, Japanese completely denied their cruelty, which made us furious.

    intense

    （strong and great）

    intense hatred

    intense pain

    intense fierce / competition

    intensity 强度

    intensify

    intense bombing 强烈轰炸

    intensive bombing 集中轰炸
