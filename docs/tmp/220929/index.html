    vanity

    vanity 虚荣

    (van = empty)

    vanish = disappear

    arrogance 自大

    narcissism 自恋

    egotism 自大

    conceit 自负

    tran. 她最大的缺点是虚荣

    -> Vanity is her Achilles' heel.

    我怕疼 be sensitive to pain

    frail = fragile

    be of a piece

    be of a piece 表里如一

    = be in agreement with

    = be in accordance with

    = be in line with

    = be in conformity with

    = be in tune with

    = be in harmony with

    benign

    1. kind and gentle
    2. harmlesss 良性（肿瘤）

    表不确定

    he was something of an athlete.

    kind of / sort of

    She has settled down in USA or something.

    留下深刻的印象

    (sb) be struck by/ with sth

    (sth) leave a deep mark on sb

    (sth) leave/produce/create/make a deep impression on sb

    Trans:这部电影精法的拍摄技巧给观众们留下了深刻的印象

    -> The highly sophisticated filming technique struck the audience

    uncanny

    uncanny 不可思议的，难以置信的

    magical, incredible, miraculous, marvelous, inconceivable

    The dogs bear an uncanny resemblance to their owners after they have lived together for a long time.

    sip

    sip 小口喝

    nurse 慢慢地喝（通常指酒精饮料）

    slurp 发出声音地吃、喝

    swig (back) = knock back = chug = drink like a fish 牛饮

    wash down 喝……将其吞下

    drink excessively 纵酒

    喜欢/不喜欢

    think a lot of sb.

    = think much of sb.

    = care for sb.

    = like sb.

    think little of sb.

    = dislike sb.

    grim 露齿笑

    go/be broke 破产，没钱

    = be down and out 穷困潦倒，一蹶不振

    = poverty-stricken

    = be in a tight budget

    = be up to my neck in debt

    = as poor as a church mouse

    = reach the excess of destitution

    = I am freaking out

    = he breaks down

    = be on verge of breakdown

    = go to pieces

    = make sb. crack up

    = curl sb. up
